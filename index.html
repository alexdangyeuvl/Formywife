The First of Many

I built this little corner of the internet just for you…

My dearest,

I built this little corner of the internet just for you. Whenever the words feel too big for a text message, or I want you to have something to return to on a quiet evening — you’ll find them here.

There are no grand announcements, no audience. Just you, and whatever I have needed to say. I hope that each time you open this page you feel, even for a moment, exactly how much you are loved.

Forever yours,
Your love

A Quiet Tuesday

Nothing special happened today — which is exactly why I wanted to write…

My love,

Nothing special happened today — which is exactly why I wanted to write. I’ve noticed I tend to save big feelings for big occasions, and forget to say the small ones out loud.

So here it is: I am so grateful for ordinary days with you. The coffee, the quiet, the way you laugh at your own jokes before you finish telling them. All of it.

I love you more than I remember to say.

Yours always,
Your love
